    • In game server rules!

    1. Don't be a dick (same as TG one)

    2. Keep all ooc talk in looc or in in-game ooc chat if you can, but we are not super harsh on mistakes.

    3. No ERP

    4. Try to RP a character. We are aiming for a medium or high RP environment.

    5. For PVP: You can only kill other players if they, or you, are being obviously evil.

    6. Do not kill or steal from AFK players.

    7. No communicating to others about the current round unless it is in a text chat in the official pentest discord in spoilers.

    8. You need to be 18+ to play on this server.

    Discord rules!

    1. Don't be a dick to other users. That includes no harassing, doxxing, callouts, wishing harm and the likes. Try to keep the salt level at a reasonable level and take serious arguments, including politics, to private messages.

    2. Don't use slurs, or offending terms. The policy is zero tolerance on the server.

    3. Don't use channels outside of their intended purpose.

    4. Don't abuse pings, especially towards admins.

    5. Don't break any of Discord's terms of service.

    6. Don't try to find loopholes and abuse edge cases to break or come close to breaking the rules. Repeatedly toeing the line will be considered as breaking a rule.

    7. No NSFW content.

    8. If you want to make a comment in a discord about an ongoing pentest round, make sure it is in spoilers in a public channel in this discord. Do not livestream a current ongoing round.

    9. No breaking metacommunication rules from other servers.

    10. No Doxxing; Do not discuss or disclose any user's personal information from: name, age, address, or other irl information without their consent. This includes jokes about Doxxing.

    Pentest is a thrill-packed part round-based, part marathon, exploration and roleplaying game. It is all set against the backdrop of a whimsical metal spacefaring machine masquerading as a spaceship, with charming spritework designed to represent the sci-fi setting and it's dangerous undertones. Have fun, and survive with your crew!
